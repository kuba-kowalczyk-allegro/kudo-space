# Next Stage Feature Themes

1. **Team Spaces & Access Control** – Support multiple boards scoped to teams or projects with invite-based membership and role-aware permissions.
2. **Richer Profile Pages** – Introduce lightweight user profiles highlighting photo, role, and recent kudos to add context and surface expertise.
3. **Engagement Signals** – Enable emoji reactions and threaded comments to keep recognition flowing without overwriting the original message.
4. **Celebration & Digest Automation** – Deliver weekly digests and milestone badges (e.g., first kudo, 10 kudos received) to reinforce positive rituals.
5. **Insight Dashboard** – Provide leaders with analytics on participation, themes, and AI usage to quantify cultural impact and guide interventions.
6. **Mobile-First Enhancements** – Polish responsive layouts and share-from-mobile flows so distributed teams can capture kudos anywhere.
